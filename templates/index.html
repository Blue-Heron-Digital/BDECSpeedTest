Many factors may impact upload/download speed tests, at any given test period. This speed test measures how well your current connection works between your computer/device and the testing server itself – providing a general measure of how fast (or slow) your internet connection is. This test doesn't necessarily mean that this is the bandwidth you should always expect between you and anywhere else. Two things to ensure the most accurate test results:

  1. Minimizing the "noise" during an internet speed test, which is what the several tips above help you do, certainly contributes to a more accurate speed test result.
  2. Testing and retesting typically provide the best results
